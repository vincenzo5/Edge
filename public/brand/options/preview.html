Edge Logo Options

Four directions for the Edge brand mark. SVGs are inlined here so the preview works in Cursor and offline file opens. You can also serve this via the dev server at /brand/options/preview.html.

Option A — Signal E

Current direction · Information edge

Green "E" with ascending signal bars — reads as live data and the information advantage. Closest to the existing brand kit.Recommended default

Option B — Razor Wedge

Literal edge · Breakout energy

Angular E with a sharp green wedge — suggests a cutting edge, breakout move, or trend inflection. Bold and distinctive at small sizes.

Option C — Apex Corner

Chart-native · Analytical

Axes meeting at a green apex with a trend line — pure chart geometry. Feels Bloomberg-terminal, research-first, and calm.

Option D — Bevel E

App icon · Product mark

Rounded badge with a beveled E and green middle bar — strongest as a favicon / dock icon. Subtle depth without skeuomorphism.
